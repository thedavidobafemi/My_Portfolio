I'm David Obafemi — a media strategist and creative director with a track record of building and scaling brand presences across Nigeria's most competitive digital landscapes. I work with brands of all sizes to craft content strategies that don't just look good — they convert.

My work spans social media strategy, AI-powered automation, content creation, and brand identity. Whether I'm directing a Reel, building a content calendar, or setting up automated marketing workflows, I bring both creative instinct and strategic discipline to the table.

Every brand I've touched has gotten a tailor-made strategy — no templates, no guesswork. From fashion academies and food brands to bridal boutiques and lifestyle communities, I understand how to position a brand and keep it growing.
